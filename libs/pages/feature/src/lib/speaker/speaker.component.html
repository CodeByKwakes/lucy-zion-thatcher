Lucy Zion Thatcher is a bold, charismatic sexual health and empowerment speaker with extensive experience in sex health advising, and comprehensive sexuality education. With a passion for promoting healthy relationships, gender equality, and body positivity, Lucy is committed to empowering individuals to embrace their sexual wellness and pleasure.

With a strong background in consent education and sexual intimacy skills, Lucy equips audiences with the knowledge and tools to navigate healthy sexual relationships. Her inclusive approach ensures LGBTQ+ individuals feel seen, heard, and supported as well as those who are neurodivergent. Lucy has ADHD and knows the value of having her difference acknowledged and accommodated.

Lucy is a dedicated advocate for sex positivity, promoting a culture that embraces diverse sexualities and identities as well as exploring sexual identity through the lens of her varied intersectionality. Through her engaging and informative presentations, Lucy encourages audiences to explore their sexual empowerment and discover what brings them pleasure.

As a versatile speaker, Lucy is ideal for various audiences, from school/university campuses to corporate conferences and community organizations.

Her dynamic and interactive style ensures participants walk away with a deeper understanding of consent and its importance, the value of a comprehensive sex education and the benefits of developing a sex positive mindset.
